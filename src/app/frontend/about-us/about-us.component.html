About Us

Vendease is a platform that makes procurement easier and more transparent for Hotels and Restaurants in Africa. On the Vendease platform, businesses can order anything they want from verified vendors at the best possible prices.

Vendease helps businesses grow by financing their procurement - we know how important cashflow is to a business, so we purchase goods for businesses at no initial cost and let you pay us when you’ve made a profit. Our customers love us because we buy anything a business needs, give flexible pricing options, offer next day delivery and provide spending analytics to help them grow.
